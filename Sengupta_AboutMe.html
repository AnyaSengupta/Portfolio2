This made me efficient on and top of my grades, as I can balance academics with my extracurricular activities such as clubs and basketball.

Through basketball and receiving the Most Valuable Player award, I learned the importance of having others depend on me. Not only is this essential in sports but also in the Technology Industry when you have meetings and collaborate with others. I persistently work on my leadership skills as I am an officer for the Environmental Technology Club and Build Club.

As you can see, I possess various characteristics that will be a significant addition to anyone, but my most valuable trait is my passion to continuously learn. I know in this industry, you are always learning on the job, but that may be the greatest part for me in the future. I am so excited for all the opportunities in the future!
